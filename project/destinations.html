Denali National Park

Denali National Park is home to North America's tallest mountain and offers visitors stunning alpine landscapes, glacier-fed rivers, and incredible wildlife viewing opportunities. Travelers can explore scenic trails, enjoy guided bus tours, or experience flightseeing tours over snow-covered peaks. Wildlife such as grizzly bears, moose, caribou, and Dall sheep can often be spotted throughout the park.

Aurora Borealis in Fairbanks

Fairbanks is one of the best places in the world to witness the Aurora Borealis. During the winter months, vibrant green, purple, and red lights dance across the night sky. With minimal light pollution and clear skies, visitors have excellent viewing opportunities from late August through April.
