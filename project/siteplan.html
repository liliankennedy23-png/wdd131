Website Planning Document

Site Name

Alaska Travel & Adventure Guide

This name clearly represents a travel-focused website dedicated to exploring Alaska. It reflects adventure, tourism, and informational content for visitors interested in Alaska’s destinations and experiences.

Optional domain: alaska-adventure-guide.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide travelers with helpful information about visiting Alaska. The site will highlight destinations, activities, travel tips, and recommended experiences to help users plan an unforgettable trip.

Scenarios

  • What are the best places to visit in Alaska for first-time travelers?
  • What outdoor activities are available in Alaska during different seasons?

Color Schema

  • Deep Blue (#003366): Used for headers, navigation, and footer.
  • Light Gray (#f5f7fa): Used for background and content sections.
  • White (#ffffff): Used for text backgrounds and cards.

Typography

  • Montserrat: Used for headings.
  • Open Sans: Used for body text and paragraphs.

Wireframe

Mobile View:

  • Header with site title
  • Navigation menu
  • Main content stacked vertically
  • Footer with references link

Desktop View:

  • Header with horizontal navigation
  • Main content displayed in sections
  • Footer at the bottom of the page
